# Traffic Fines and Penalties in Germany

## Overview

Germany uses a three-tier penalty system:
1. **Verwarngeld (Warning fine)**: Minor offenses, up to €55
2. **Bußgeld (Administrative fine)**: More serious offenses, €60 and above
3. **Straftat (Criminal offense)**: Severe violations prosecuted under criminal law

## Punktesystem (Points System) — Flensburg

The Kraftfahrt-Bundesamt (KBA) in Flensburg manages the points register.

| Points | Consequence |
|---|---|
| 1–3 points | Warning (Vormerkung) |
| 4–5 points | Written warning + recommendation for voluntary seminar |
| 6–7 points | Written warning + mandatory seminar participation |
| 8 points | **License revocation (Entziehung der Fahrerlaubnis)** |

Points expire after different periods:
- 1-point offenses: **2.5 years**
- 2-point offenses: **5 years**
- 3-point offenses (criminal): **10 years**

## Speeding Fines (as of 2024)

### Within Urban Areas (Innerorts)
| Excess Speed | Fine | Points | Driving Ban |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 10 km/h | €30 | 0 | – |
| 11–15 km/h | €50 | 0 | – |
| 16–20 km/h | €70 | 0 | – |
| 21–25 km/h | €115 | 1 | – |
| 26–30 km/h | €180 | 1 | 1 month* |
| 31–40 km/h | €260 | 2 | 1 month |
| 41–50 km/h | €400 | 2 | 1 month |
| 51–60 km/h | €560 | 2 | 2 months |
| 61–70 km/h | €700 | 2 | 3 months |
| Over 70 km/h | €800 | 2 | 3 months |

*Driving ban applies if this is the second offense within 12 months at 26+ km/h over.

### Outside Urban Areas (Außerorts)
Fines are generally 20–30% lower than urban violations for the same excess speed.

## Red Light Violations

| Violation | Fine | Points | Driving Ban |
|---|---|---|---|
| Red light (under 1 second) | €90 | 1 | – |
| Red light (over 1 second) | €200 | 2 | 1 month |
| Red light with endangerment | €320 | 2 | 1 month |
| Red light causing accident | €360 | 2 | 1 month |

## Alcohol and Drug Offenses

| BAC Level | Fine | Points | Driving Ban |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.5–1.09‰ (first offense) | €500 | 2 | 1 month |
| 0.5–1.09‰ (second offense) | €1,000 | 2 | 3 months |
| 0.5–1.09‰ (third offense) | €1,500 | 2 | 3 months |
| 1.1‰ and above | Criminal offense | 3 | 6+ months |
| Drug influence (any amount) | €500+ | 2 | 1 month+ |

**Note**: For drivers under 21 or in the probationary period (first 2 years), the BAC limit is **0.0‰** (absolute zero tolerance).

## Mobile Phone Use
- Using a handheld phone while driving: **€100 + 1 point**
- With endangerment: **€150 + 2 points + 1 month ban**
- Causing an accident: **€200 + 2 points + 1 month ban**
- Cyclists using phones: **€55**
