# Speed Limits in Germany

## General Speed Limits

Germany's speed limits are governed by the Straßenverkehrs-Ordnung (StVO), the road traffic regulations.

### Urban Areas (Innerorts)
- **Default speed limit: 50 km/h**
- Applies to all roads within city and town boundaries, marked by yellow town-name signs
- Many residential areas have **30 km/h zones** (Tempo-30-Zonen), marked with special signage
- Near schools and kindergartens, limits may be reduced to **20 km/h** during certain hours
- "Spielstraßen" (play streets / living zones) have a limit of **walking pace (Schrittgeschwindigkeit)**, approximately 4–7 km/h

### Outside Urban Areas (Außerorts)
- **Default speed limit: 100 km/h** for cars and motorcycles
- Applies to all roads outside town boundaries that are not Autobahn
- Vehicles with trailers: **80 km/h** (can be raised to 100 km/h with special approval)
- Buses carrying standing passengers: **60 km/h**
- Buses without standing passengers: **80 km/h**

### Autobahn
- **No general speed limit (Richtgeschwindigkeit: 130 km/h recommended)**
- The 130 km/h is an advisory speed — not legally binding, but exceeding it can affect liability in accidents
- Many sections have posted speed limits (often 100, 120, or 130 km/h)
- Temporary speed limits may be displayed on electronic signs (Wechselverkehrszeichen)
- Minimum design speed to use the Autobahn: vehicle must be capable of at least **60 km/h**

## Speed Limits for Specific Vehicle Types

| Vehicle Type | Urban | Rural | Autobahn |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cars | 50 km/h | 100 km/h | No limit (130 advisory) |
| Cars with trailer | 50 km/h | 80 km/h | 80 km/h |
| Motorcycles | 50 km/h | 100 km/h | No limit (130 advisory) |
| Trucks (3.5–7.5t) | 50 km/h | 80 km/h | 80 km/h |
| Trucks (>7.5t) | 50 km/h | 60 km/h | 80 km/h |
| Buses | 50 km/h | 80 km/h | 80 km/h |

## Temporary and Conditional Limits
- **Wet weather**: Some Autobahn sections post lower limits "bei Nässe" (when wet), typically 80 km/h
- **Construction zones (Baustellen)**: Limits often reduced to 60–80 km/h, with narrow lanes
- **Environmental zones**: Speed may be reduced for noise or emission control
- **Night-time restrictions**: Some areas impose lower limits between 22:00 and 06:00
